Hi, I am Yanan Li. I am currently a researcher at Zhejiang Lab. Beforing joining Zhejiang Lab, I completed my Ph.D. in College of Computer Science from Zhejiang University, co-supervised by Prof. Yueting Zhuang and Prof. Donghui Wang. My research interests include computer vision, machine learning, zero-shot learning, few-shot learning.
